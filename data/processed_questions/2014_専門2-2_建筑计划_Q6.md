# 問題 1 Question 1

図に示す小学校は, 「開かれた学校」の一例として捉えることができる. 図を参照し, (1) (2) の両方に答えなさい.

The figure below shows a plan of an elementary school which can be seen as a built example of "open design schools" in Japan. Examine the figure and answer the following questions, both (1) and (2). 

(1) 日本の教育施設計画の分野で「開かれた学校」のテーマで議論された視点を2つあげ, 図の小学校においてそれらの視点がどのように実現されているか, 1つの視点についてそれぞれ300字以内で説明しなさい.

"Open design schools" concept included two points of views when it was discussed in the domain of educational facilities planning in Japan. Describe these viewpoints and explain how they were materialized in the school shown in the figure below in 150 words per each viewpoint. 

(2) 各々の視点の実現によって生じることが予想される問題点を視点ごとに100字以内で説明しなさい。

Explain the presumable negative effects of introducing the viewpoints in a school design in 50 words for each viewpoint.
