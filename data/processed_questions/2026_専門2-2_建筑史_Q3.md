(4) その建築の歴史的・文化的・社会的な特徴など、(2)、(3)以外の特徴を3行程度で説明しなさい。

(3) Explain the features of the building from the perspective of space, design and floor plan, in about three lines. 

(4) Explain the features of the building other than (2) and (3) (e.g. historical, cultural, and social characteristics) in about three lines.

B. Cologne Cathedral (Cologne, Germany)
